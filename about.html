Who We Are

SP Landscaping LLC is a family-owned and locally operated business with over 20 years of experience focused on practical, clean, and lasting outdoor improvements. We take pride in dependable communication, efficient work, and quality craftsmanship on every project.

From patios and retaining walls to fencing, lawn work, and landscape upgrades, our goal is simple: make the property look better, function better, and feel finished the right way.

What We Focus On

  • Hardscaping and paver work
  • Fencing and gate projects
  • Landscape cleanup and upgrades
  • Clear communication and reliable scheduling
